about us

what we envision

We envision a vibrant and united African Diaspora community, bound together by shared unity of our numbers, values, mutual support, and a commitment to uplifting each other, our homelands and a better life in our respective countries of residence.
Our vision is to create a powerful network of solidarity and financial empowerment, leveraging the collective strength of the Diaspora to drive positive change in our lives in our countries of residence and across Africa.

about us

what we envision

THE FOURTH BOOK’s mission is to foster a sense of belonging, financial empowerment and unity among Africans in the Diaspora, enabling them to contribute meaningfully to the development of Africa while achieving financial security and stability.
Through our innovative contributions and payout model, THE FOURTH BOOK aims to harness the strength of our community to make a significant impact, one member at a time; guaranteed.
